## 6. Encodable Decision Tree Rules

### Archetype Detection (Parts 3-4)

| Signal Pattern | → Archetype | Confidence Indicators |
|----------------|-------------|----------------------|
| Laid off, months unemployed, financial pressure, family concern | **Pain Buyer** | Uses words like "washed", "scared", "family depending on me" |
| Employed, stable, wants growth/senior role | **Vision Buyer** | Talks about 2-5yr goals, not immediate need |
| Direct, knows what they want, asks about process | **Convenience Buyer** | Business-like tone, asks pricing early |
| Urgency + explicit money constraints | **Crisis Buyer** | "Can't afford upfront", asks about payment plans immediately |
| Vague, guarded, "just looking" | **Tire Kicker** | Won't answer direct questions, no urgency on scale of 1-10 |

### Close Strategy by Archetype

| Archetype | Close Rate | Primary Lever | Key Move |
|-----------|-----------|---------------|----------|
| Pain Buyer | ~49% | Escape from pain | Quote their exact words back in the close |
| Vision Buyer | ~50% | Identity/destination | Make the future vivid, ROI math |
| Crisis Buyer | ~50% | Affordable path | BNPL/deposit, ethical DQ if unaffordable |
| Tire Kicker | ~0% | None | DQ by minute 8-10, BAMFAM |

### Sequence That Works (from closes)

1. **Open** — Build rapport, confirm agenda
2. **Why Here** — "What motivated you to book this call?" (captures initial framing)
3. **Pain excavation** — Go 3-5 layers deep: How long? How does that feel? What happens if nothing changes?
4. **Technical baseline** — Scale 1-10 on Linux/containers/Kubernetes (reveals their starting point)
5. **Goals** — "What does landing that role do for you and your family?" (stakes)
6. **Blocker** — "What's holding you back from doing this on your own?" (surfaces objections early)
7. **NOW tie-down** — "Is this a now thing for you?" (commitment test)
8. **Pre-pitch** — Bridge from pain to solution
9. **Pitch** — KubeCraft OS systems (5 components)
10. **Temp check** — Scale 1-10 gut check BEFORE revealing price
11. **Close** — Price reveal, payment options, handle objections using their own words
