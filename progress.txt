### Known Issues

### Patterns Discovered
- Small local models (Phi-3.5-mini) achieve parity with larger models when given domain context
- RAG reduces token usage 66-88% by retrieving only relevant script sections
- Stateless approach (Phase 4) is simpler and more reliable than stateful (Phase 3)
- Sentence-ending punctuation is a reliable analysis trigger
- 5 trigger conditions interact — modifying one affects others (buffer_manager invariant)
